Our brasseries are warm and welcoming with comfortable spaces where you can gather with friends, family or colleagues for a leisurely lunch, our seasonal, two course set menu or dishes from our extensive à la carte menu. Many of our restaurants have great little bars and lovely outside areas where you can stave off the hunger until dinner with a glass of wine, a beer or a refreshing cocktail.

Located on the Clifton Triangle at the top of Park Street, Wahaca Bristol brings the taste of Mexican market eating to the West Country. Alongside award-winning street food, we also serve some seriously tasty margaritas – grab a drink, play some table football and tuck into some freshly smashed guacamole. We invited one of the original pioneers of Bristol’s renowned urban art scene, Will Barras (of Scrawl and Street Sketchbook fame), to leave his mark on the restaurant by covering our walls with some floor to ceiling trademark pieces
