Pata Negra is a bustling, Spanish tapas & wine bar in the heart of Bristol’s Old City. Literally meaning, ‘Black Hoof’ the name, Pata Negra, refers to the finest Jamon Iberico, produced from free range Black Iberian pigs, fed on acorns (Bellota), and cured for 36 months.
